<h3>003 &nbsp;—&nbsp; HOW I WORK</h3>

<br/>

> **Methodical over fast.**  
> A rushed solution is just a future bug with extra steps.

> **Depth over breadth.**  
> I'd rather fully understand one concept than skim ten.

> **Readable over clever.**
